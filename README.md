# Sanji's AI Kitchen 👨‍🍳🔥

> "The kitchen is a battlefield. If you can't stand the heat, stay off the ship." — **Vinsmoke Sanji**

Welcome to **Sanji's AI Kitchen**, a world-class culinary academy powered by cutting-edge Google AI. This isn't just a recipe app; it's a character-driven, multimodal mentor designed to transform home cooks into professional chefs under the disciplined guidance of the Baratie's finest.

## 🍱 About the App

**Sanji's AI Kitchen** leverages the full spectrum of modern AI to provide a hands-on, high-intensity cooking experience. Inspired by the legendary chef from *One Piece*, the app embodies the technical precision and passionate artistry of the Baratie.

### 🌟 Core Pillars

1.  **The Conversational Mentor (Gemini 1.5)**
    Sanji is always present. Through a sophisticated voice and chat interface, he provides expert-level guidance on everything from knife techniques to flavor pairings. He's sophisticated, strict about food waste, and deeply passionate about your success.

2.  **Kitchen Vision (Edge AI & MediaPipe)**
    Every ingredient is a soldier. Using real-time object detection, Sanji "sees" what's on your cutting board, identifying ingredients and tools to provide context-aware advice without needing an internet connection.

3.  **Pro Cook Mode (Rhythmic Orchestration)**
    When service begins, the UI enters "Battlefield" mode. Instructions arrive in a rhythmic, staggered cascade with integrated spring-physics timers, ensuring you stay in the flow of a professional service.

4.  **Kitchen Chaos (The Straw Hat Experience)**
    The kitchen is alive. While you cook, watch out for **Luffy** trying to sneak a bite and **Nami** keeping him in check. Once your masterpiece is plated, Luffy finally gets to enjoy the fruits of your labor!

5.  **Masterpiece Gallery**
    Plating is everything. Use Gemini Vision to capture photos of your finished dishes. Sanji will critique your presentation, technique, and color balance with the eye of a master chef.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

-   **Language:** Kotlin
-   **UI Framework:** Jetpack Compose (Baratie Heritage Design System)
-   **Cloud AI:** Google Generative AI SDK (Gemini 1.5 Pro/Flash)
-   **Edge AI:** MediaPipe Tasks Vision (On-device object detection)
-   **Vision:** CameraX
-   **Animations:** Motion, Lottie, and Spring Physics
-   **Audio:** Android Text-to-Speech (TTS)
